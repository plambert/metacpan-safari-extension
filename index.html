What is it?

The MetaCPAN Safari Extension is an extension which automatically redirects any *.cpan.org page to the equivalent *.mcpan.org page, thereby always showing you MetaCPAN even if you click or open a link to CPAN.

Why?

I'm tired of clicking the "MetaCPAN" link on the top right of CPAN pages, and I prefer the MetaCPAN layout.

How do I use it?

Download the extension here: https://plambert.net/metacpan-extension/MetaCPAN.safariextz

Double-click it to install it.

Frequently Asked Questions
